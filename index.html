PROJECTS

Studiocracy

Studiocracy is a social media portal and marketplace for artists to connect locally with collectors near them. As founder of this startup, I did full-stack development work in Ruby on Rails, as well as managing a team of designers and developers with Agile development.

My duties also included promoting the company through external networking and marketing, including close collaboration with ASU's entrepreneurship office in the form on mentoring and financial support. Studiocracy is currently slated to launch Summer 2015.

ArkHumanity

ArkHumanity is a nonprofit that mines Twitter for tweets suggesting self-harm or suicidal thoughts and provides them to crisis hotlines for outreach and further assessment. The project involves machine learning and natural language processing to optimize filtering of the tweets.

On the team, I'm beginning development on the web application that will allow professionals and volunteers to log-in, view, and interface with the tweets served up by the program. I develop full-stack using Django. The project has significant support from ASU, including a $10,000 grant as winner of the university's Changemaker Challenges. Development will continue into the next year as we forge partnerships with additional organizations.

MyMeta

MyMeta is a Chrome extension that allows users to view a custom Metacritic score based only on publications and critics that they list. This JavaScript app ties a list of the user's favorite publications to their Google account and generates a custom aggregate score using JQuery based only on those publications' reviews.
